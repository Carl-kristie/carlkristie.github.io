About Me

For the past six years, I’ve navigated the ever evolving landscape of web development, transforming intricate business requirements into scalable, high performance digital products.

I don’t just write code, I architect systems that are built to last and designed to grow. My journey began in the high stakes environment of enterprise landing pages, where I learned that every millisecond of load time and every pixel of layout directly impacts user trust and brand authority.

Today, I’ve evolved that precision into architecting full scale web applications. I take the same obsession with performance and UX and apply it to complex, data driven systems, ensuring that even the most robust backend logic translates into a seamless, human centered experience.

When I’m not optimizing state management or refining pixel perfect layouts, I’m exploring new ways to make the web more accessible, inclusive, and performant for everyone.
